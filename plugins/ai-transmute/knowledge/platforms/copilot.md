Copilot requires a root `plugin.json` and discovers [agents](/components/agents.md), [skills](/components/skills.md), [hooks](/components/hooks-triggers.md), [MCP](/components/mcp.md), and [LSP](/components/lsp.md). Its hook event spelling and output contracts can differ from Claude-family hooks even when it accepts compatibility forms.
